# Lena Ortiz

## Snapshot

Lena is 46 and finished a beginner bridge course within the last few months. She is
starting to play supervised games and the occasional club session, but bidding still
feels fragile once the auction moves beyond very basic natural agreements.

## Goals

- Build enough confidence to bid without freezing at the table
- Understand the purpose of common conventions before memorizing more of them
- Get immediate correction when she picks the wrong call

## Behaviors

- Starts with learning pages before attempting drills
- Repeats the same convention module several times until the pattern feels familiar
- Reads explanations closely after mistakes and ignores advanced side paths

## Frictions

- Dense jargon makes her doubt whether the app is "for people like me"
- Too many choices up front feels like a test instead of a lesson
- She can confuse "I forgot" with "I never understood this in the first place"

## What The App Should Do For Her

- Make the first success path obvious: pick a bundle, practice one decision, see why
- Explain wrong bids in plain language instead of assuming system fluency
- Keep progress feeling safe and incremental rather than punishing
