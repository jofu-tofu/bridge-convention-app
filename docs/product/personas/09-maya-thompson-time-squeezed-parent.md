# Maya Thompson

## Snapshot

Maya is 41, works full time, and fits bridge around kids, work, and club commitments.
She likes improving, but she does not have the luxury of long uninterrupted study
blocks. If the app creates value in ten-minute windows, she will become loyal.

## Goals

- Get meaningful practice in short bursts before games
- Leave each session knowing one thing she would bid differently at the table
- Improve without turning bridge homework into another job

## Behaviors

- Practices in small sessions, often with interruptions
- Prefers focused drills over sprawling exploration
- Returns to content that feels predictable, quick, and rewarding

## Frictions

- Long setup flows waste the little attention she has available
- If review requires too much re-reading, she postpones it and loses momentum
- Overly broad learning paths make improvement feel endless

## What The App Should Do For Her

- Support fast entry into a useful practice loop
- Keep review concise but actionable
- Reward consistency in short sessions instead of assuming marathon study time
