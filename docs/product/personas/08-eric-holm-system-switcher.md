# Eric Holm

## Snapshot

Eric is 45 and reasonably advanced, but his current pain is not beginner confusion.
He and his partner are changing systems and need help internalizing which responses,
thresholds, and continuations have actually changed rather than just reviewing generic
convention theory.

## Goals

- Rebuild confidence after a system transition
- Distinguish old system reflexes from the new agreement set
- Drill changes until the new defaults feel automatic

## Behaviors

- Approaches practice with a migration mindset: "what do I need to unlearn?"
- Is willing to spend time on configuration if it leads to more accurate training
- Wants explanations that surface the implication of system context

## Frictions

- Generic convention summaries are not enough when the problem is system-specific drift
- He gets annoyed if the app cannot help him isolate what changed
- Ambiguous language around forcing or strength ranges creates distrust

## What The App Should Do For Him

- Make system-dependent meaning legible and easy to compare mentally
- Help him focus on changed branches, not relearn everything from scratch
- Support disciplined retraining for an experienced partnership
