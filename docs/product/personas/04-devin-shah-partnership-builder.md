# Devin Shah

## Snapshot

Devin is 38 and is the more organized half of a serious club partnership. He is not
looking for generic improvement as much as he is trying to eliminate agreement drift
between himself and his regular partner before sectional and regional events.

## Goals

- Verify that both partners mean the same thing when they click the same bid
- Practice decision points that produce expensive misunderstandings
- Build a tighter shared language around convention structure

## Behaviors

- Uses the app right before partnership discussion sessions
- Treats mistakes as evidence of ambiguous agreements, not just personal errors
- Wants to point a partner at the exact bundle or module that needs work

## Frictions

- Solo practice is less useful if it cannot be turned into partnership alignment
- He gets frustrated when explanations are accurate but not specific enough to settle a debate
- He dislikes flows that feel educational but not agreement-grade precise

## What The App Should Do For Him

- Make convention meanings explicit enough to support partnership discussion
- Encourage repeatable drills on exact auction branches that matter
- Reinforce precision without becoming unnecessarily academic
