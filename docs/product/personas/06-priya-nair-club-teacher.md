# Priya Nair

## Snapshot

Priya is 52 and teaches supervised play lessons while also mentoring newer club
players one-on-one. She is evaluating the app partly for her own use, but mostly as a
teaching asset she can recommend without needing to apologize for the pedagogy.

## Goals

- Find explanations she would be comfortable putting in front of students
- Use learning pages to reinforce lesson material between classes
- Recommend a practice path that meets mixed-skill learners where they are

## Behaviors

- Reads copy and review text more critically than a typical player
- Looks for clean conceptual explanations before she looks for feature breadth
- Mentally maps app flows onto common classroom misunderstandings

## Frictions

- She rejects tools that are technically right but pedagogically sloppy
- Abrupt jumps in difficulty make her reluctant to recommend the product
- If the app seems to reward memorization without understanding, she loses interest

## What The App Should Do For Her

- Explain conventions in a way she could confidently echo in class
- Make the learning path legible for weaker students
- Pair correctness with teaching quality, not just scoring
