# Bridge Rules Sources

Authoritative references for bridge rules implemented as tests in this codebase.

## Primary Sources (Official/Semi-official)

- **Laws of Duplicate Bridge 2017** — World Bridge Federation (WBF). The definitive rulebook.
  - https://www.worldbridge.org/rules-regulations/laws/
  - Covers: auction procedure (Laws 17-39), play procedure (Laws 40-47), scoring (Laws 73-78)
- **ACBL Convention Chart** — American Contract Bridge League
  - https://www.acbl.org/convention-charts/
- **SAYC System Booklet** — ACBL Standard American Yellow Card
  - https://www.acbl.org/learn_page/how-to-play-bridge/how-to-bid/

## Secondary Sources (Reference)

- **bridgebum.com** — Convention encyclopedia
  - Hand evaluation: https://www.bridgebum.com/high_card_points.php
  - Distribution points: https://www.bridgebum.com/distribution_points.php
  - Play rules: https://www.bridgebum.com/how_to_play_contract_bridge.php
  - Conventions: stayman.php, gerber_convention.php, dont.php, bergen_raises.php
- **Wikipedia: Contract bridge** — https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Contract_bridge
- **Wikipedia: Bridge scoring** — https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bridge_scoring
- **Cornell scoring reference** — https://pi.math.cornell.edu/~belk/scoring.htm

## How to Resolve Ambiguity

1. Check WBF Laws of Duplicate Bridge first
2. Check ACBL SAYC booklet for bidding conventions
3. Check bridgebum.com for convention-specific details

## Known Coverage Gaps

Rules NOT yet implemented (for future sessions):

- Irregularities and penalties (insufficient bids, leads out of turn, revokes) — Laws 24-32, 48-64
- Claim and concession rules — Laws 68-71
- Alerts and announcements — Laws 40, 75
- IMP scoring and matchpoint conversion
- Rubber bridge scoring (rubber bonus, honors, above/below the line)
- Advanced conventions beyond Stayman/Gerber/DONT/Bergen
- Competitive auction rules (overcall requirements, takeout double criteria, balancing)
- Opening lead conventions and defensive carding signals
