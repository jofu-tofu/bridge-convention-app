# Noah Reed

## Snapshot

Noah is 34 and found the app through a bridge forum, a lesson recommendation, or a
friend. He is curious, comfortable with software, and willing to explore, but he is
still deciding whether this is a tool he will actually return to or eventually pay for.

## Goals

- Understand the value proposition within the first session
- Sample one convention deeply enough to trust the product
- Decide whether the app is more useful than static notes or videos

## Behaviors

- Clicks around quickly and forms impressions fast
- Will try both learning and practice views before committing
- Expects the free experience to feel real, not like a crippled demo

## Frictions

- If the first few screens feel abstract, he bounces
- Confusing premium boundaries make the product feel evasive
- He will not invest in setup before he sees concrete instructional value

## What The App Should Do For Him

- Deliver a clear first-session payoff with minimal friction
- Show enough feedback quality to justify deeper use
- Make the transition from curiosity to habit feel natural
