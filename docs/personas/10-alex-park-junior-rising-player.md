# Alex Park

## Snapshot

Alex is 19, plays a lot online, and is improving quickly with coach and mentor input.
He is younger than most club players, extremely comfortable with software, and highly
motivated by visible improvement. He can absorb detail, but only if the tool feels
serious and fast.

## Goals

- Accelerate from talented junior to dependable competitive partner
- Use structured repetition to compress the learning curve
- Review mistakes in a way he can discuss with a coach afterward

## Behaviors

- Learns quickly from interactive correction and pattern repetition
- Is happy to explore multiple modes and settings if they add training value
- Treats software as part of a broader improvement stack, not a one-off lesson

## Frictions

- Slow or overly cautious UI makes the app feel behind his expectations
- He disengages if explanations are shallow or repetitive
- He wants a sense that practice is building real competitive strength

## What The App Should Do For Him

- Feel responsive, modern, and worth repeated use
- Offer enough explanation depth to support coached improvement
- Turn repetition into visible skill-building instead of rote clicking
